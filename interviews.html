Captain Kamal Hossain

A decorated army officer, Captain Kamal Hossain shares his experience of leading a battalion during the liberation war.

Biographical Details: Born in 1945, Kamal Hossain enlisted in the army in 1965 and played a key role in military operations during the war.

Interview Summary: Kamal Hossain describes the horrors of battle, the loss of comrades, and the eventual victory that led to the independence of Bangladesh.
